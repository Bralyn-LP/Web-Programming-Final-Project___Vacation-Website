Balkans Package

Featuring: Croatia, Albania, Romania

Croatia

Explore the stunning landscapes and rich history of Croatia. From beautiful coastlines to historic cities, Croatia has it all.

Albania

Discover the hidden gems of Albania, with its diverse landscapes, ancient ruins, and warm hospitality.

Romania

Immerse yourself in the charm of Romania, from the medieval castles of Transylvania to the vibrant culture of Bucharest.


Dubrovnik

Walk amongst the buildings and walls of the “Old Town Dubrovnik” where the cobble-stone structures show an older time.

Take a guided tour through Dubrovnik and take in the beauty of the old coastal town. The city itself is regarded as a World Heritage Site, and showcases beautiful medieval architecture. In addition it has a very clear pearl sanded coast to admire from the land, or make for a scenic kayaking session.


Walls of Ston

Take a trek along the Walls of Ston; a 5.5km long pentangular wall linking Ston to Mali Ston.

Constructed in the 14th century the Walls of Ston is one of the longest preserved fortifications in the world.


Pula Arena

Visit the Pula Arena, a 1st-century Roman amphitheatre.

Constructed in the 1st century the Pula Arena is one of the best preserved amphitheatres, and the 6th largest in the world!


Vaser Valley Forestry Railway

Take a train ride through the Carpathian mountains on a steam-powered train! Afterwards enjoy a stop in Paltin for food and fun.

The Vaser Valley Forestry Railway as the name would imply was fromerly used to transport logs from forsest to mill, and has been in operation since 1932.


Bran Castle

Roam the halls of the Bran Castle, the most iconic of the Romanian castles.

Built in 1377 Bran Castle now serves as a museum displaying art and furniture collected by Queen Marie, with guided tours availble to show its many rooms, works, and event the smaller local building such cottages, barns, and water mills.


Bucharest

Visit Bucharest, the capital city of Romainia


Porto Palermo Castle

Come see the well preserved stone castle of Porto Palermo and take in its seaside view.

Originaly constructed in the 15th century, and rebuilt/fortified in the 19th century the Porto Palermo Caste makes for a historic fortress.


The Blue Eye

Swim in the natural water spring renowned as Blue Eye for its deep blue depths as the spring contains a deep underwater cave in its center.

Berat Castle

Walk through Berat Castle a fortress built around the town of Berat, made up of several Byzantine chruches, and Ottoman mosques.

Reconstructed in the 13th century the modern day Berat Castle shows it wear in a charming fasion as portions of its original 5th century construction show its age.
