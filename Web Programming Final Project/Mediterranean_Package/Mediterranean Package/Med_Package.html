The Mediterranean Journey


Journey across the countries of the Mediterranean region!


Learn more about the Mediterranean countries of Greece, Italy, and France!


Greece

Experience the ancient history and beautiful islands of Greece. Visit Athens for its historic sites and enjoy the crystal-clear waters of Santorini and Mykonos.

Italy

Italy is known for its rich history, art, and cuisine. Explore iconic landmarks like the Colosseum in Rome, the canals of Venice, and the art treasures of Florence.

France

Experience the romance and culture of France. Visit Paris for the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum, explore the vineyards of Bordeaux, and relax on the French Riviera.
